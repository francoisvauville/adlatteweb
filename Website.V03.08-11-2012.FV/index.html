FAQs

  • Q. What is AdLatte?

    AdLatte is a mobile advertising platform that provides users with advertisements according to their interests. When you watch the ads and interact with the tasks you are rewarded certain amount of virtual money that can be used within our affiliates or be paid cash into your personal bank account. The AdLatte service is free and can be enjoyed all around the world.

  • Q. How does it work?

    First, download the AdLatte App onto your mobile phone from the App Store or Google Play. Second, create your own AdLatte account and provide us some basic information and interests.

    Third, receive advertisements customized for you and enjoy them whenever and wherever you are. Fourth, get rewarded for all the interactions you’ve done on AdLatte. Use this money at our various affiliated stores or get it transferred to your personal bank account .

  • Q. How much does it cost?

    AdLatte is a free service for anyone who is interested in advertisements.

    Note that if you’re not in a Wi-Fi coverage area, you will need to pay for the costs for your mobile internet. For a more enjoyable AdLatte experience we recommend that you have a flat rate data plan (standard flat fee per month for using the mobile internet) to avoid extra charges on your mobile payments.

  • Q. What's in it for me?

    AdLatte is a user friendly service that has been created to offer you advertisements that will satisfy your interests. Check out the latest ads from your favorite stores, brands, and retailers.

    offers exclusive promotional events to users

    become a proactive consumer to share thoughts and comments on these products and services

    we share with you the money from our advertisers for your engagements

  • Q. How can I earn money through AdLatte?

    AdLatte rewards you for all the interactions and engagements you do on AdLatte. Whether its copywriting, writing a comment, participating in an event or downloading applications, you will receive credits that can be transferred into real cash or used at affiliated stores.

  • Q. Why do I earn money for this?

    By creating an AdLatte account, you have a voice to communicate with advertisers as a smart consumer.

    There are lots of benefits by becoming an AdLatte user, be the first to get the latest information on events, promotions, discounts, and more.

    We believe that you are acceptable to receive the money from AdLatte since advertisers are willing to communicate and interact with you.

  • Q. How do I check how much I have earned?

    You can go to ‘My Page’ on the AdLatte application to check your balance and all the advertisements you’ve seen.

  • Q. when do I get paid?

    When you’ve reached the minimum threshold of XX dollars you can register to withdraw your money on "My Page" and we’ll transfer it to your bank account.

  • Q. How much does it cost?

    AdLatte is a free service for anyone who is interested in advertisements.

    Note that if you’re not in a Wi-Fi coverage area, you will need to pay for the costs for your mobile internet. For a more enjoyable AdLatte experience we recommend that you have a flat rate data plan (standard flat fee per month for using the mobile internet) to avoid extra charges on your mobile payments.

  • Q. Will you charge me for using this in the future?

    No you can be assured that AdLatte will never charge you in the future for any reasons. As AdLatte grows globally with more users, you will get more advertisements and various benefits.

  • Q. How do I know you will look after my data?

    AdLatte is a business model that is made by you, our users. The more satisfied our users are, the more money we will earn. To ensure that all of your personal data is kept safe with us we maintain your anonymity will all other companies, and do not allow access to your identification to any other parties.

    AdLatte is critical in gaining and maintaining your trust.

  • Q. How do I de-subscribe?

    You may delete your account whenever you need through the AdLatte application. Go into Settings/Account/Information/Delete Account and follow through on the deleting process.

  • Q. Are there any restrictions to joining AdLatte?

    There are no restrictions on joining AdLatte. However we do not allow double accounts by the same mobile number. And you cannot join AdLatte within the course of 3 months from deleting your previous account.

  • Q. How do I contact you?

    For further questions you can contact us through our website and we will be happy to provide you with the information you need. We are always keen to know your thoughts on our services and advice for improvement.
